#+TITLE: clef
#+PROPERTY: header-args :tangle yes

Clef (.clef) live coding via nREPL.  Requires =clef-mode= in =lisp/ext/clef-mode.el=
(autoloaded from =config.org=) and =cider= (clojure component).  SLY remains
available for SBCL; it is disabled inside Clef buffers only.

#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
(with-eval-after-load 'clef-mode
  (add-hook 'clef-mode-hook #'enable-paredit-mode)
  (add-hook 'clef-mode-hook #'aggressive-indent-mode))
#+END_SRC
